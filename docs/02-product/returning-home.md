# Returning Home

Returning is enough — the return experience proves it. Only coarse, device-local context is used
(a `saelis-last-visit` timestamp in localStorage; nothing is stored server-side and nothing is
counted): first visit → _"Welcome to a quiet place of your own."_ · returning after a day or more
→ _"It is good to see you again."_ · after roughly two weeks → _"We can begin again."_ · returning
the same day → nothing at all.

Never shown: days absent, missed days, streaks or resets, "we missed you," dependency language,
or any claim that Saelis waited emotionally. Familiarity is never overstated; Saelis does not
claim feelings.
