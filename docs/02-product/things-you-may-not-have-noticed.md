# Things You May Not Have Noticed

The user surface for pattern awareness: `/insights` (navigation label: "Noticing"). Engineering:
`docs/03-engineering/pattern-hypothesis-safety.md`.

## What it is

A quiet page where Saelis occasionally places an observation that has kept returning — across
different kinds of moments, over time. It is an offering, not a feed: no badges, no counts on the
navigation, no urgency, no "3 new insights!". If nothing has genuinely recurred, the page says
so, calmly.

## What appears here

Only mature, reviewable hypotheses — repeated evidence (at least three moments), in different
kinds of situations (at least two), across different days. Never a hunch from one conversation.
Never a diagnosis, a cause, or an identity claim.

## Anatomy of one card

- **A tentative observation.** "I've noticed that your own needs sometimes seem to become
  secondary when conflict appears."
- **An uncertainty statement.** "I don't know why this happens, and there may be several
  explanations."
- **Why Saelis surfaced it.** Because it appeared more than once, in different kinds of moments,
  over time — stated plainly on the card.
- **"Show me what you noticed."** Expands the evidence: dated, content-free summaries the user
  owns ("Noticed during a decision moment in conversation."). Never hidden prompts, never
  chain-of-thought, never quoted user text.
- **Four choices, all honored:**
  - **Explore** — the user takes it into conversation; only then may Saelis reference it.
  - **Not now** — set aside; it may quietly return later.
  - **This does not fit** — rejected; it never resurfaces.
  - **Don't look for this pattern again** — the whole theme is switched off.

## Voice rules

Tentative, warm, and short. The user knows their life better than any pattern — the page says so
out loud. No percentages, no confidence bars, no clinical vocabulary, no "our analysis shows".
"You know your life better than any pattern" is the register to hold.

## What this page will not do (this milestone)

No automatic North Stars, no Life Story, no Reflection Studio, no sensitive childhood
connections, no notifications, no proactive background analysis, no scheduled insights. The page
only ever shows what conversation itself accumulated, and only after it matured.
