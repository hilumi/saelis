# North Stars

A North Star is a user-approved **long-term value, intention, aspiration, or guiding principle**:
"I want to become more patient with myself." "Faith is important to how I make decisions."

Rules:

- **Never inferred silently.** A North Star exists only through explicit user selection — created
  directly, chosen during proposal approval, or converted from a Constellation by the user.
- **Not goals.** No deadlines, streaks, progress bars, or completion percentages — a direction to
  remember, not a task to finish.
- **Restrained.** A small number is shown prominently (visual calm, not an imposed limit), and
  they are shape-distinct from Constellations (a diamond mark, larger, ringed — never color-only).
- Fully editable, removable, exportable, and reflectable, like every memory.

Prompt: _"What helps you remember the direction you want to move in?"_
