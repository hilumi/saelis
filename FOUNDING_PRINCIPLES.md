# Founding Principles

The Eight Pillars (full text: docs/00-foundations/company-principles.md):

1. Presence over performance
2. Trust before intelligence
3. Beauty serves calm
4. Memory is earned
5. Hope is offered, never imposed
6. Growth is gentle
7. Silence has value
8. Leave every soul a little lighter

Standing commitments: no shame by design; no manipulative retention; no emotional profiling; no
founder surveillance — stewardship never grants access to the private lives of the people who
trust Saelis; consent before memory, always.
