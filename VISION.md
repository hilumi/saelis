# Vision

Come as you are. Leave a little lighter.

Saelis exists to prove that technology can leave people feeling more human than when they
arrived: a quiet place to feel understood, think clearly, and find what comes next. Success is a
person closing the app a little lighter — never a session-length chart. The full articulation
lives in docs/00-foundations/the-book-of-saelis.md and docs/00-foundations/north-star.md.
