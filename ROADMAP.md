# Roadmap

- **v0.1 Foundation** — shipped
- **v0.2 Light Engine** — shipped
- **v0.3 Awakening** — shipped
- **v0.4 Living Sky** — shipped
- **v0.5 Constellations & Stewardship** — shipped
- **v0.6 Home** — shipped
- **v0.7 Saelis Core: Relational Intelligence Foundation** — shipped: moment-aware
  response posture, linguistic mirroring (form only), humor/challenge calibration, adaptive
  preferences with transparent confidence, pattern hypotheses with "Things you may not have
  noticed", deterministic post-validation enforcement
- **v0.8 Web Beta Readiness** — shipped (this release): onboarding, trust pages, feedback
  categories, mobile + reliability hardening, deployment runbook. Legal review of Terms/Privacy
  required before public launch
- **v0.9 (proposed)** — provider-side safety classification behind the urgent-override contract;
  managed rate limiting/idempotency; Gentle Breeze (expiring context) per the Memory Charter;
  richer pattern evidence sources (arrivals, horizon) behind the same maturity gates
- **Later** — Seasons, Quiet Days, The Garden, Weather Within, Reflections, Last Light; data
  export beyond memories; internationalized crisis resources; legal/privacy review before beta

Never on the roadmap: streaks, engagement mechanics, emotional scoring, founder access to
private conversations, or ads.
